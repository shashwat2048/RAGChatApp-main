What is a Startup?
A startup is a young company founded by entrepreneurs to develop a unique product or service and bring it to market.

Startup Funding Stages:
1. Pre-seed: Initial capital from founders, friends, and family
2. Seed funding: Early investment to prove concept
3. Series A: Funding for optimizing products and user base
4. Series B: Scaling the business
5. Series C and beyond: Further expansion and preparation for IPO

Key Startup Metrics:
- Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)
- Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
- Lifetime Value (LTV)
- Burn Rate
- Churn Rate

Common Startup Challenges:
- Funding and cash flow management
- Market competition
- Talent acquisition
- Product-market fit
- Scaling operations

Startup Success Factors:
- Strong founding team
- Unique value proposition
- Market size and timing
- Sustainable business model
- Adaptability and resilience
